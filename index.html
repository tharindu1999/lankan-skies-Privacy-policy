Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 21, 2026

Lankan Skies is a mobile game that respects your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles information when you install and play. The short version: we do not collect personal information, we do not run ads, and your gameplay data stays on your device.

📋 Information We Collect

Lankan Skies does not ask you to create an account and does not collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, precise location, camera data, microphone data, or payment information.

The app saves game progress and preferences locally on your device so the game can function correctly. This may include:

  • High score, coins, and total distance
  • Furthest route progress and games played
  • Sound, haptic, and graphics settings
  • Tutorial status and selected character, bike style, and trail
  • Mission progress, best-run statistics, and photo count

This gameplay information is stored locally on your device. We do not receive it, sell it, or use it to identify you.

🔒 Device Permissions

Lankan Skies requests the following Android permissions:

VIBRATE

Used to provide optional haptic feedback during gameplay. This permission does not collect personal information.

INTERNET

Included because the app is built with a mobile web runtime and may require standard platform connectivity support. The current game does not use user accounts, online leaderboards, advertising, in-app purchases, or analytics tracking.

📷 Photo Mode

Lankan Skies includes a photo mode that can create an image from the game screen when you choose to capture it. This feature does not use your device camera. The image is created from the game canvas and is saved or handled by your device only when you choose to use the feature.

We do not upload, transmit, or access these images in any way.

☁️ Android Backup

Depending on your Android device settings, the operating system may back up app data through your Google account. This backup is managed and controlled by Android and your Google account settings — not by us. We do not directly access, store, or interact with that backup data.

🔗 Third-Party Services

Lankan Skies does not include advertising SDKs or analytics SDKs in the current release.

Google Play and Android may process information related to app installation, app updates, crash reports, device diagnostics, and store activity according to Google's policies and your device settings.

⚙️ How We Use Information

Locally stored gameplay information is used exclusively to provide game features on your device:

  • Save and restore game progress
  • Remember your settings between sessions
  • Unlock in-game items and track missions
  • Improve the play experience on your device

🤝 Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share personal information from Lankan Skies. The app does not transmit your local gameplay progress to any server.

🗑️ Data Retention and Deletion

Game progress and settings remain on your device until you:

  • Clear the app data through Android Settings
  • Reset the device storage for the app
  • Uninstall the app

If Android backup is enabled on your device, some app data may be restored by Android according to your Google account backup settings.

👶 Children

Lankan Skies is designed as a general-audience game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can review the issue.

🛡️ Security

We take a data minimization approach: gameplay progress is kept on your device and the app avoids account registration, advertising tracking, and analytics tracking in the current release.

No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, but the app is designed to avoid collecting personal information where it is not needed.

📝 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes, when new features are added, or when legal requirements change. Any update will be published at this URL with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

✉️ Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or how Lankan Skies handles data, please contact us at your support email address on the Google Play Store listing.
